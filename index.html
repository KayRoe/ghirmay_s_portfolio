BIOGRAPHY


Welcome, my name is Senait Ghirmay and everyone calls me Sen, and this is my portfolio.

My toolbox consists of HTML, Javascript, CSS, SASS, Adobe Creative Applications such as Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign, After Effects, Adobe XD, Cinema 4D and more...

Being an artist at heart, I enjoy making designs that are visually appealing, whether they be for logos, business cards, or online interfaces.

I find front-end programming to be equally fascinating in its technical difficulties as it is in its aesthetics. From my understanding, development goes beyond only writing code and also includes creating interactive digital worlds.
I'm more than just a developer; I'm also a designer.

I'm ecstatic about the possibility of combining my talents and interests to produce designs and beneficial websites for potential clients. Join me as we develop together on this exciting trip.
